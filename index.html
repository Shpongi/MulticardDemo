Set Up Your Multicard

Follow these simple steps to create and configure your Multicard. The process is straightforward and only takes a few minutes.

  1. Enter your company name and contact details.
  2. Select your campaign name and preferred currency.
  3. Choose card design, branding assets, and message template.
  4. Set recipient details (single upload or bulk import).
  5. Define amount, delivery date, and redemption preferences.
  6. Review all entries, confirm, and submit the form.
